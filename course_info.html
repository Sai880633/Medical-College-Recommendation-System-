Medical Courses Information

MBBS
Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)

MBBS is a professional undergraduate degree awarded to students who have completed the study of medicine and surgery in India. The duration of the MBBS course is five and a half years, including a one-year internship program.

BAMS
Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS)

BAMS is an undergraduate degree program in Ayurvedic system of medicine. The duration of the BAMS course is five and a half years, including a one-year internship program.

BDS
Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

BDS is a professional undergraduate degree program in dentistry. The duration of the BDS course is four years, followed by one year of internship.

BHMS
Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS)

BHMS is an undergraduate degree program in the field of Homeopathy. The duration of the BHMS course is five and a half years, including a one-year internship program.
